US Market Sentiment is concerning ‚Äî 23.89% negative sentiment, compared to only 4.25% globally.

Issues identified are not global, but highly localized to specific US regions.

Root causes may include:

Product quality defects

Counterfeit goods entering distribution

Return policy exploitation

Misalignment of customer expectations

## ‚úÖ üìå Project Summary

Using a relatable example (coffee preparation), the project makes Lean methodology easy to understand and highly applicable to daily operations, manufacturing, service environments, and business processes.
