  ## 2. üìå Project Description -Cost Optimization Strategies in Recruitment & Training  
A detailed and business-focused analysis presenting cost-optimization strategies in recruitment and training, designed to help organizations improve hiring efficiency, reduce training costs, and maximize ROI.

  ## üìå Project Summary

This presentation provides a strategic guide for reducing HR operational expenses while maintaining high workforce quality. The project breaks down the key cost drivers, identifies inefficiencies, and delivers a structured roadmap to optimize recruitment and training using:
Process automation
Internal hiring frameworks
E-learning platforms
Standardized onboarding
Data-driven performance measurement

The analysis helps HR leaders and organizations create a sustainable, cost-efficient talent ecosystem without compromising employee experience or training effectiveness.
